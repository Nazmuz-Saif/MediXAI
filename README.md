## 🔬 Research Overview

Healthcare systems face significant challenges in processing handwritten prescriptions and providing personalized health insights. **MediXAI** proposes a multimodal AI pipeline that combines:

- 📷 **OCR** — Automated extraction of text from handwritten prescriptions
- 🧬 **BioBERT** — Medical NLP for understanding clinical terminology
- 👁️ **YOLO / CNN / LSTM** — Visual recognition and sequence modeling
- 🌐 **Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** — Multimodal understanding of prescription images
- 🔍 **Explainable AI (XAI)** — Transparent, interpretable predictions for clinical trust
- 🔒 **Federated Learning** — Privacy-preserving distributed model training
